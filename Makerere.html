About Makerere University

Makerere University, founded in 1922, is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Africa. Located in Kampala, Uganda, it offers world-class education in various fields of study. The university is committed to research, innovation, and community development.

Our mission is to provide high-quality education, foster innovation, and contribute to the development of Uganda and Africa.

Academic Programs

Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering

Our Software Engineering program offers cutting-edge knowledge and skills needed to build and manage software systems.

Master of Public Health

This program equips students with advanced skills in public health, preparing them to tackle health challenges across Uganda and the world.

Engineering Programs

We offer various engineering programs, including civil, mechanical, and electrical engineering, all aimed at developing future leaders in the engineering sector.

Admissions

Applying to Makerere University is easy! We offer undergraduate, graduate, and diploma programs in a variety of fields. Applications are open throughout the year for different programs, and the university provides scholarships for deserving students.

To apply, visit the official admissions portal.

Campus Life

At Makerere University, we believe that education extends beyond the classroom. Our vibrant campus life offers students opportunities to engage in extracurricular activities, leadership programs, and community service.

Students can join various clubs, societies, and sports teams, making it an enriching and fulfilling experience during their university years.
